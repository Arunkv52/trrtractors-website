Need for AutoTrac™?

Introducing for the 1st time in India, AutoTrac™ is an automated vehicle guidance system. It provides the operator with hands-free straight path guidance, enhancing in-field productivity & greatly reducing operation fatigue.

Benefits of AutoTrac™?

Hands-free steering experience for the operator, while the tractor is guided on a straight path. It reduces overlaps, saves time and fuel in row crops especially during planting/seeding.
